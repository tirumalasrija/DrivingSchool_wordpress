Our Goal

  • Here at United Driving School our main goal is safety and customer satisfaction.To make sure we meet our safety goals, we have our vehicles go through a 32-point inspection. This inspection includes safety points like tire pressure, fluid levels, brakes, etc. Our vehicles also have a second brake system which puts the instructor in control just in case our students make a mistake.
  • Our instructors are certified, licensed, and fully accredited to meet the demands of the state of Michigan. Instructors go through a rigorous background and driving record check. Our goal is to provide the excellent service and total student satisfaction.
  • For any more questions or concerns, please navigate to our F.A.Q.’s page. If you still can’t find an answer to your questions please feel free to give us a call.
