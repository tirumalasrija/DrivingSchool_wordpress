Our history

Established in 1998, Traffic Signs Company has grown into one of the foremost sign making organisations in the country.
  • 2011

    At our Company we offer the highest quality products for those customers who require more than the standard traffic control products.

  • 2012

    We voluntarily submitted our policies and procedures to the Committee for independent review and became the first manufacturer to be included in the Good Index.

  • 2013

    We acquired several companies in a strategic move to enhance our position on the global market.

  • 2014

    We acquired a US-based company with an innovative portfolio of products for use under supervision. We divested some businesses in North America and Oceania to North Castle Partners.
