Our services

Traffic Signs Company evolved its traffic service offerings to provide full-service traffic control, in addition to the latest and most effective traffic products outlined below:

Traffic management

We are able to provide your company with not only certified traffic control personnel but are also able to offer personnel with Level 1, 2 or 3 First Aid to ensure safety on your work site.

  • Integrated Traffic Management
  • Engineer Sealed Traffic
  • Public Communications and Stakeholder Relations
  • Incident Management Services

Emergency response

We provide an emergency response 24 hours a day 7 days a week for electrical faults or knocked down lighting columns.

Working closely with the emergency services, our clients, and other stakeholders we strive to maintain the safety, integrity and restoration of service within the agreed time.

We have specialist resources, systems and procedures to ensure that we provide the best possible service safely, quickly and with the minimum disruption to the public.
